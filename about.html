Meet the founder

Edward Ward

Founder & CEO

Ed has spent the last decade building and scaling technology ventures.

Most recently, as COO of Polkastarter, the first and leading crypto launchpad, he helped secure the company's exit in 2024. He continues to advise and invest in early-stage Web3 through Poolside, an accelerator and fund.

Before crypto, Ed launched Le Wagon coding bootcamp in London in 2016, helping build it into the world's leading coding school, and later joined SURFnCODE, an AI venture studio founded by the original creators of Skype, to run its founder programme. He began his entrepreneurial career in 2013 with the Veglo Commuter X4 bike light, funded on Kickstarter and distributed in 14 countries before its sale to Oxford Products Ltd.

Ed brings an operator's perspective to Big Room Tech, having built, scaled, and exited across three technology waves.
