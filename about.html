About Big Room Tech

Big Room Tech is a strategic and venture-acceleration advisory firm focused on early-stage and growth-stage technology businesses, with a particular emphasis on artificial intelligence and advanced digital solutions.

We partner with ambitious founders and leadership teams to transform ideas into commercially viable, scalable products. Our work spans the full journey — from initial strategy through to market positioning, product development, and commercial execution.

Based in Miami, FL, we work with clients across North America, Europe, and beyond, bringing deep expertise in AI product strategy, venture advisory, and digital transformation.
